Smallest Titanics of Special Forms

This first list was first compiled by David Broadhurst

  • The smallest pair of titanic primes is
    • 10^999+7
    • 10^999+663
    as proven by Preda Mihailescu and Giovanni La Barbera.
  • The smallest pair of titanic twin primes is
    • 10^999+1975081
    • 10^999+1975083
    as proven by Heuer Daniel (11 Oct 1999).
  • The smallest titanic Sophie Germain pair is
    • 10^999+2222239
    • 2*10^999+4444479
    as proven by Giovanni La Barbera.
  • The smallest semi-titanic Sophie Germain pair is
    • 10^999+1043119
    • 5*10^998+521559
    as proven by Giovanni La Barbera.
  • The smallest titanic 4-fold arithmetic progression is
    • 10^999+2059323
    • 10^999+2139213
    • 10^999+2219103
    • 10^999+2298993
    as proven by David Broadhurst.
  • The smallest titanic 3-fold arithmetic progression is
    • 10^999+61971
    • 10^999+91737
    • 10^999+121503
    as proven by David Broadhurst.
  • The smallest titanic Cunningham chain of 2nd kind is
    • 10^999+2209041
    • 2*10^999+4418081
    as proven by David Broadhurst.
  • The smallest semi-titanic Cunningham chain of 2nd kind is
    • 10^999+547137
    • 5*10^998+273569
    as proven by David Broadhurst.

David Broadhurst's eleven primes above were proven by nine runs of Certifix. Minimality was proven by 1,149,496 PrimeForm tests.

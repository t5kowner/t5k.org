98. MERSENNE NUMBERS.—In Nat. Acad. Sci., Proc., v. 34, Mar. 1948, p. 102-103, Professor H. S. UHLER gives details of his proof (completed 27 Nov. 1947) that M 193 is composite. Thus he brought to a conclusion work begun in 1944 (MTAC, v. 1, p. 333) when the characters of just six of the M p , p = 157, 167, 193, 199, 227, 229, were unknown. He has now shown that all of these are composite. See also MTAC, v. 1, p. 404; v. 2, p. 94, 341. Professor Uhler's final summary of some of the facts concerning the 55 Mersenne numbers is as follows:
